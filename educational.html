Master of Arts National Level: CUET-PGOfficial Link: NTA Exams Portal / NTA Website Short Description: A single, all-India computerized entrance test conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA) for admissions into postgraduate programs across Central, State, and participating Deemed/Private Universities.Eligibility:Educational Qualification: You must hold a Bachelor’s degree (like B.A., B.Sc., B.Com, B.Tech, or equivalent) from a recognized university. Minimum Marks: Typically requires a minimum of 50% to 55% aggregate marks in graduation for General/OBC candidates, with a 5% relaxation (or simple pass standard) for SC/ST/PwD categories. (Note: Precise percentage cuts vary dynamically by destination university).Age Limit: There is no upper age limit set by the NTA to sit for the exam.

NIMCET NIT ADMISSIONS 2026

The admission into the MCA programme for the year 2026-27 in above 11 NITs and 02 IIITs is based on the Rank obtained in NIMCET-2026 only.
